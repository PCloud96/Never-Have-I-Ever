Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

1. Overview

Cloud Motion Lab ("we", "our") operates Never Ever: Party Game ("the App"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Data We Collect

The App does not require account creation and does not collect personal information such as your name, email, or location.

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

- Game preferences and settings

- Player names entered during gameplay (stored temporarily in memory, not persisted)

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

Google AdMob: Displays advertisements in the free version. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve relevant ads. See Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy

Apple StoreKit: Handles subscription purchases. Apple processes payment information according to their privacy policy at https://www.apple.com/privacy/

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Data collected by AdMob is governed by Google's privacy policy.

5. Data Storage

All app preferences are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. No data is transmitted to our servers. We do not operate any backend servers.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is rated 17+ and is not intended for children under 17. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is no personal data for us to delete or export. You can reset all local app data by deleting and reinstalling the App. You can opt out of personalized ads in iOS Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at cloudmotionlab@gmail.com
